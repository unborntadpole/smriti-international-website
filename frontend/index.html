About Us

Delivering reliable manpower solutions with trust and efficiency

Smriti International is a trusted manpower and recruitment service provider, specializing in connecting skilled and unskilled workers with companies across various industries. We focus on delivering quality talent, ensuring that both employers and job seekers achieve their goals efficiently.

With expertise in domestic and overseas hiring, we provide end-to-end solutions including recruitment, documentation support, and workforce management. Our commitment to professionalism and transparency makes us a preferred partner for businesses.
